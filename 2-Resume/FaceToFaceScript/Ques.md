# 🎯 Face-to-Face Guidance Conversation (SDE-1 Focused)

## 1️⃣ Opening — Asking for Time
“Sir, can I take 5–6 minutes of your time? I wanted some focused guidance based on where I am right now.”

---

## 2️⃣ Context — Purpose of the Discussion
“This time I mainly wanted your feedback on my resume and one project, and to understand what’s still missing for Tier-1 SDE-1 readiness.”

---

## 3️⃣ Resume Review — Getting Honest Feedback
“I’d really appreciate if you could review this and tell me what looks strong, what looks weak, and what feels average from a Tier-1 perspective.”

---

## 4️⃣ Project Walkthrough — Positioning the Project
“This is one of my main projects. I built it to show how I approach problem-solving and structure things end-to-end, not just to display features.”

---

## 5️⃣ Gap Analysis — What’s Missing
“Based on what you know about me — DSA, this project, and my background — what do you think is missing right now for SDE-1 roles at Tier-1 companies?”

---

## 6️⃣ Strengths — What to Double Down On
“And on the positive side, what do you think I’m already doing right that I should continue doubling down on?”

---

## 7️⃣ Referral Readiness — Understanding the Bar
“From this center, when do you personally feel comfortable referring someone to your network? What signals or consistency do you look for?”

---

## 8️⃣ Closing — Ending the Conversation Cleanly
“This gives me a very clear direction. I’ll work on these gaps and update you once I’ve improved. Thanks a lot for the guidance.”








# 🎯 Face-to-Face Guidance Conversation (SDE-1 Focused)

## 1️⃣ Opening — Time Maangna
“Sir, kya main aapke **5–6 minutes** le sakta hoon? Mujhe thodi **focused guidance** chahiye based on where I stand right now.”

---

## 2️⃣ Context — Discussion ka Purpose
“Is time main mainly apne **resume aur ek project** par aapka feedback lena chahta hoon, aur ye samajhna chahta hoon ki **SDE-1 readiness** ke liye abhi kya missing hai.”

---

## 3️⃣ Resume Review — Honest Feedback
“Main really appreciate karunga agar aap resume dekh ke bata sakein ki **kya strong lagta hai**, **kya weak**, aur **kya average** lagta hai from a **product-company hiring perspective**.”

---

## 4️⃣ Project Walkthrough — Project ko Kaise Position Karna
“Ye mera ek main project hai. Maine ise sirf features dikhane ke liye nahi, balki apna thinking process aur approach dikhane ke liye build kiya hai.”
---

## 5️⃣ Gap Analysis — Kya Missing Hai
“Aap jo mujhe jaante hain — meri background, ye project, aur overall profile — uske basis par aapko kya lagta hai ki **strong SDE-1 roles** ke liye abhi kya missing hai?”

---

## 6️⃣ Strengths — Kis Par Focus Rakhoon
“Positive side par, aapko kya lagta hai ki main **kya cheezein sahi kar raha hoon** jin par mujhe **aur zyada focus continue** karna chahiye?”

---

## 7️⃣ Referral Readiness — Bar Samajhna
“Is center se, aap personally **kab comfortable feel karte ho** kisi ko apne network mein refer karne ke liye? Aap kaunse **signals ya consistency** dekhte ho before doing that?”

---

## 8️⃣ Closing — Clean End
“Isse mujhe kaafi clear direction mil gaya. Main in gaps par kaam karunga aur improve hone ke baad aapko update karunga.  
**Thank you so much for the guidance, sir.**”
